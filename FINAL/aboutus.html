Flor’s Canteen History

Flor’s Canteen started in 2004 as a small family-run business by Flordeliza Sarmiento. Over the years, it became a go-to place for students, workers, and locals because of its delicious, affordable, and unique meals. Many customers keep coming back because of the home-cooked taste and warm service.

Mission

To serve affordable, high-quality meals that bring comfort and satisfaction to every customer.

Vision

To become a well-loved and trusted canteen known for consistency, taste, and excellent service.
